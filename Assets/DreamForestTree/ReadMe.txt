================Dream Forest Tree===============
Requires Unity 2019.4.28 or higher.


Designed for Linear Color Space.
This pack includes:
- 4 trees (pine based);
- 2 simple bushes;
- 4 grass textures;
- 4 ground textures;
- 1 Post-Processing profile;
- 1 demo scene.

- tris count ~3000-5000;
- support Unity wind;
- support terrain billboards.

Please Note: All trees and bushes made with 'Unity Tree Creator' and full editable.
